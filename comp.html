Hex PRO

There are some guidelines when entering your bot into the pro competition.

  • Your bot must not tamper with your opponents bot.
  • Your bot must run each step in reasonable time.
  • Your bot may not skip its turn.
  • Your bot may not use more than 1mb memory. (if this is you, you are doing it wrong)
  • Your code should be reasonably well commented / understandable.
  • Your code can be written in javascript, mocha or coffee.
